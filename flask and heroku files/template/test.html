Saving Lifes
By Measuring Severity Of Patients

As the covid-19 cases are increasing by huge amount every day, hence it is becoming difficult for hospitals to allot beds to patients. The aim of this project is to measure severity of patients and hence to give preference to patients with higher severity so that they could get beds before there condition gets critical.
